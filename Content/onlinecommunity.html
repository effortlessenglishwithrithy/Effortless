About Me

I am

Hi folks! My name is Som Rithy, and I'm the author and creator of English Solutions with Rithy and Effortless English with Rithy. Recently I am a university student at year 4 of the College Media and Communication at the University of Cambodia.

Currently, I am a teacher of English of the Effortless English with Rithy and English Solutions with Rithy and I'm also known as a content creator on YouTube channels, Facebook pages, Telegram channels and groups.

I'm thrilled to embark on this new and exciting journey of using my program to facilitate the way people speak English with confidence and strength. From my earliest childhood, I have always loved language and communication. I see language as a tool for thought and idea expression as well as a way to communicate with people from every aspect of life and understand a variety of cultural backgrounds. This belief has increased my motivation to support people as they pursue their English language learning goals.

Let's get started with your fluency journey, keep practicing and using English with effective methods!
